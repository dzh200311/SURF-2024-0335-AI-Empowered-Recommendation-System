Welcome to EasyEat Delivery Platform!

Welcome to the EasyEat delivery platform! Here are some simple guides to help you better use our services.

1. Registration and Login

  • 1.1 Register an Account: If you are new to EasyEat, you need to register an account. Click the "Register" button on the homepage, fill in the required information such as your email, and create an account.
  • 1.2 Login to Account: If you already have an EasyEat account, click the "Login" button on the homepage and enter your email and password to log in.

2. Ordering Process

  • 2.1 Browse Restaurants: After logging in, you can browse the list of restaurants we support. Click on the restaurant name to view its menu and reviews.
  • 2.2 Choose Dishes: Choose dishes in your favorite restaurant and add them to the shopping cart.
  • 2.3 Place an Order: After confirming the order, click on the shopping cart to go to the checkout page. Choose a delivery address and select a payment method to complete the order.

3. View Orders

  • 3.1 Order Status: After logging in, you can view your order history and order status on the personal center page.
  • 3.2 Order Details: Click on the order number to view order details, including the list of dishes, delivery address, and other information.

4. Account Management

  • 4.1 Personal Information: On the personal center page, you can view and edit your personal information.
  • 4.2 Change Password: If you need to change your password, you can find the "Change Password" option on the personal center page.

5. Contact Customer Service

If you have any questions or issues, please feel free to contact our team. You can reach us by the following means:
Email: service@EasyEat.com

6. Feedback and Suggestions

We highly value your feedback and suggestions. If you have any comments or suggestions about our services, please tell us by the following means:
Email: service@EasyEat.com

Thank you for choosing EasyEat Delivery Platform, and enjoy your meal!
